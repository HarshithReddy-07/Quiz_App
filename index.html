General instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

The Questions Palette displayed on the left side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols :

Unanswered
Answered
Mark for review
Answered and Mark for review

Note: Answered and Mark for review Questions Will be evaluated

Navigation to Question :

"previous" buttton → go to previous question

"next" buttton → go to next question

Answering to Question :

To select you answer, click on the one of the options.

To deselect your chosen answer, click on the Clear Response button

To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option

To mark a question for review, click on the Mark for review button
